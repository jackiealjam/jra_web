I had the hardest time choosing because this is a big thing, my very first website I will be creating. It took me a few days to think about it, I felt like it was taking ages for me to choose not that I had a lot of things I wanted as a webpage but you know I had no idea how big or small it would be. So there was an evening where my niece was telling "you know aunty all of your siblings and cousins all talk about us nieces and nephews like"my niece!" and "my nephew"...just like grandma Grace does"... I laughed and said "ommgggg I feel like my mom right now".... Then I asked her and my sister Dion how they would feel about my webpage being about my niece Isabella. My niece ofcourse thought it was the best and started writing and sending me things, and I told dion she had to contribute some words as this was going to be about her daughter and all... And thats on my first webpage, I am very PROUD of my webpage and how it turned out. I hope you check it out!
